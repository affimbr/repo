CallMagic Tweak

Oriented towards calls either over Cellular or Internet, this tweak is designed to give the user control over the flowing features:

  • Play music during call. (implemented)

    • - choose a specific audio file from disk and play it during the call session.

    - call detection.

    - local speaker preview.

    - local audio, headphones, airpods and bluetooth handsfree support.

    - volume control.

    - live seek in file.

    - auto repeat.

    - read iTunes local playlist.

    - support for other audio players source files. (to do)

    - support for live stream apps like Youtube Live and Facebook Live background audio mixing. (to do)

  • Record call. (in progress)
  • Change voice during call. (in progress)
  • Answering machine. (in progress)
  • Incoming call view redesign. (in progress)
  • Share screen during video call. (in progress)
  • More to be added based on your requests.

This tweak was tested and found working on devices with iOS 9 up to 11.3.1 32bit and 64bit in all Video and Audio call apps (PhoneApp, FaceTime, Facebook Messenger, Skype, WhatsApp, Zoom, Viber, Hangouts, ...) . Tested on (4s,5,6,7+,X). It is still in development so you might have to wait a bit for your proffered feature.
