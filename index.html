CallMagic Tweak

Oriented towards calls either over Cellular or Internet, this tweak is designed to give the user control over the flowing features:

  • Play music during call (implemented)
  • Record call (in progress)
  • Change voice during call (in progress)
  • Answering machine (in progress)
  • Incoming call view redesign (in progress)
  • Share screen during video call (in progress)
  • More to be added based on your requests

This tweak was tested and found working on devices with iOS 9 up to 11.3.1 32bit and 64bit in all Video and Audio call apps (PhoneApp, FaceTime, Facebook Messenger, Skype, WhatsApp, Zoom, Viber, Hangouts, ...) . It is still in development so you might have to wait a bit for your proffered feature.
